關於我們

扶輪是一個實業和專業領導人士的世界性組織， 它提供了人道服務，並在所有職業裡去鼓勵高倫理道德標準， 更在世界中去協助增進親善與和平。 約近有一百二十萬位扶輪社員，隸屬於200個國家中的33,000多個扶輪社。

Rotary is a worldwide organization of business and professional leaders that provides humanitarian service, encourages high ethical standards in all vocations, and helps build goodwill and peace in the world. Approximately 1.2 million Rotarians belong to more than 33,000 Rotary clubs located in 200 countries.
